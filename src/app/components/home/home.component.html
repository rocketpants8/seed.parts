Seed.Parts

Seed.Parts allows you to securely store and distribute your Bitcoin seed phrase (or any other data). Seed.Parts encrypts your secret (using either a password, or public key encryption) and uses Shamir's secret sharing to split it into multiple components, requiring a minimum number of shares to be combined in order to decrypt the secret.

Seed.Parts does not:

  • Have any knowledge of your secret
  • Send your secret to anyone
  • Store your secret

Disclaimer

  • Shamir secret sharing is a cryptographic technique that allows a secret to be split into multiple shares, and requires a minimum number of shares to be combined in order to decrypt the secret. Splitting a secret into multiple shards brings with it a different set of risks and problems. Jameson Lopp has a good article discussing the shortcomings of Shamir's secret sharing.
  • This utility relies on other open source projects for cryptographic security, and in particular these implementations of PGP and Shamir's secret sharing
  • If you lose your shares, encryption password or private key, I cannot help you; there is no way to recover your secret.
